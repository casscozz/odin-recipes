Eggplant Lasagna

Description

This simple eggplant lasagne dish is full of flavour and comfort. It is full of cheesy goodness, tender eggplant and a rich, spicy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Brown Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Eggplant
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Passata Sauce
  • Arrabiata Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Finely dice the brown onion, carrot and celery
  3. Grate the garlic
  4. Thinly slice the eggplant
  5. Place the slices of eggplant in an oven tray, drizzle with oil and place in oven for 30 mins. Flipping the slices halfway through
  6. Heat oil in a pan and gently fry the onion. Then add the garlic, carrot and celery and fry until soft
  7. Add the passata and arrabiata sauces to the pan. Stir and let simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Line an oven try with eggplant slices, top with sauce, a lasagna sheet, and bechamel sauce. Repeat these layers.
  9. Top the final layer with grated cheese
  10. Cook in the oven for 30 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the lasagne sheets have cooked
