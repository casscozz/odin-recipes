Mars Bar Slice

Description

Delicious, easy, Aussie classic

Ingredients

  • 75g Butter
  • 150g Mars Bar
  • 1 Tablespoon Golden Syrup
  • 3 Cups Rice Bubbles
  • 250g Milk Chocolate
  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Grease and line tine with baking paper
  2. Melt the butter, two-thirds of the chopped Mars Bar and the golden syrup over a low heat
  3. Take saucepan off heat and stir through the rice bubbles and remaining mars bar
  4. Press mixture into the tin firmly
  5. Place milk chocolate and vegetable oil in a microwave safe dish and microwave until just melted, stirring every 30 seconds
  6. Spread over the slice and refrigerate until firm
