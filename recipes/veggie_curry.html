Veggie Curry

Description

This rich, korma curry is full of chickpeas and cauliflower. It is hearty, filling and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Brown Onion
  • Cauliflower
  • Chickpeas
  • Korma Paste
  • Coconut Milk
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Rice
  • Roti Bread
  • Greek Yoghurt

Steps

  1. Dice the brown onion, drain and wash the chickpeas and roughly chop the cauliflower into medium sized florets
  2. Boil the cauliflower until soft. Brown the onion and add the chickpeas
  3. Add the cauliflower, korma paste, coconut milk and tinned tomatoes.
  4. Stir and simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Serve with rice, roti bread and greek yoghurt
